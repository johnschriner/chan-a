Here's the magic system dilemma. You want a magic system, what will you chose?
For a lack of better terms, the RPG system or traditional Witches and Wizards.
The RPG system works incredibly well for combat. Elements, circles, runes that can be manipulated and formed into attacks. Its really easy to build upon it and explain how stuff works or is made. Because of its "modularity" it can be expanded in many ways.

Witches and Wizards on the other hand is all about the mystic. Recite a spell and an action happens. How it happens is not explained. Could be gravity could be any kind of elementary force. It isnt explained at that is this kind of magic's charm.

You could add gravity or force in the RPG system as elements but then it would degrade into a physics textbook as you find ways to abuse it, not so much shounen now.
