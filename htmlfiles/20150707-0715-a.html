Tell us about Towa, and if you feel any point of connection with your character.
S: I think that among the 4, she is the one I resemble the most. It's more like a process of elimination, but I think we might share the point of being too serious for our own good.I think that we could both grow up if we toned that "too serious" part down a bit.

How does your acting differs between Twilight and Towa?
S: Since Twilight was brainwashed by Dyspear, I acted her part as a completely different person.
